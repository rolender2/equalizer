Privacy Policy

Last verified: February 7, 2026

Your privacy is paramount to Sidekick Equalizer ("we", "our", or "us"). This policy explains how we handle your data when you use our desktop application and services.

1. Audio Processing

Sidekick Equalizer captures audio data solely for the purpose of providing real-time negotiation coaching. This processing is transient.

  • Transcription: Audio is sent securely to Deepgram/OpenAI for transcription. We configure these services to not store your audio or text for model training.
  • Retention: We do not retain audio recordings on our servers. Processing occurs in real-time.

2. Local Data Storage

Session transcripts and analysis history are stored locally on your device. You have full control over this data and can delete it at any time within the application.

3. Third-Party Services

We utilize the following trusted third-party services for core functionality:

  • OpenAI: Used for generating coaching advice. Data sent via API is subject to OpenAI's enterprise privacy commitments (zero retention for API users).
  • Deepgram: Used for speech-to-text transcription.

4. No User Tracking

We do not track your activity across other apps or websites. We do not sell your personal data to advertisers or third parties.
