Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enable Mic-Only Mode?

Mic-Only mode is the default for the App Store version. Just ensure your speakers are on so the microphone can pick up the counterparty's voice.

Is my audio recorded?

No. Audio is processed in real-time and transcripts are saved locally on your machine. We do not store your recordings in the cloud.

How do I delete my data?

You can delete any session directly from the "History" tab within the application. This permanently removes the local file.
